We're rolling out a modernized version of the club website. It's faster, works well on
phones and tablets, and is much easier for club volunteers to keep current.

Everything from the old site is here — repeater frequencies, the net schedule, meeting
information, license testing, and D-STAR details. If you spot anything that's out of date or
missing, please let us know at **info@nharc.org**.

73 and see you on the air!
