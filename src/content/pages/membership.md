Membership in the North Hills Amateur Radio Club is **$25 per year**. Your dues support the
club's repeaters, activities, and programs — and connect you with an active community of
amateur radio operators across Greater Pittsburgh.

## How to join or renew

There are three easy ways to pay your annual dues:

1. **By mail** — send a check to *North Hills Amateur Radio Club, P.O. Box 15333,
   Pittsburgh, PA 15237*.
2. **In person** — bring your dues to any club meeting.
3. **By Zelle** — send payment to **bank@nharc.org**. Please include your name and callsign
   in the memo.

New members and renewals are welcome any time of year. If you have questions about
membership, email **info@nharc.org**.
