The club holds **Volunteer Examiner (VE) test sessions on the second Saturday of each month**.
Testing begins at **noon**. These sessions are open to anyone seeking a new amateur radio
license or upgrading an existing one.

## Where

**Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices — The Preferred Realty (Hampton)**
4100 William Flinn Highway (Route 8), Allison Park, PA 15101

## Fees

- **$15** exam session fee — cash, paid to the VE team at the session.
- **$35** FCC application fee — for a new or upgraded license, paid **directly to the FCC**
  through the CORES system after you pass (not collected at the session).

## Reserve your seat

Please email **ve@nharc.org** (Bob Worek, AG3U) **at least 24 hours in advance** so we can
plan for your session. Walk-ins may be accommodated, but reserving ahead is strongly
encouraged. Arrive a few minutes early to complete paperwork.

## What to bring

- Two forms of identification — at least one a **photo ID**.
- Your **FCC-issued FRN (Federal Registration Number)**. This is *required* before testing —
  Social Security numbers are no longer accepted by the FCC. Register for an FRN at the FCC
  CORES website ahead of time.
- If upgrading: the **original and a photocopy** of your current license or CSCE.
- A **calculator** is permitted (no stored formulas). Bring a pen and pencils.
- **$15 in cash** for the exam session fee.
