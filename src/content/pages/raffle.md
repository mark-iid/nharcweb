The club is raffling off a brand-new **Icom IC-7300MK2** HF transceiver — 160 m through 6 m
with 100 watts on all bands, an improved receiver, video output, and a network jack.

- **Tickets are $10.00 each.**
- Only **300 tickets** will be sold.
- The drawing will be held at a club meeting once all tickets are sold, or at the **2026
  Holiday Party**, whichever comes first. **You need not be present to win.**

## How to buy tickets

- **At club meetings** and special events (paper stubs handed out on the spot).
- **By mail** — send a check payable to *North Hills Amateur Radio Club*, marked
  *Attn: Icom IC-7300MK2*, to P.O. Box 15333, Pittsburgh, PA 15237. Include your name,
  callsign, and email.
- **By Zelle** to **bank@nharc.org** — include your name, callsign, and the number of
  tickets in the comment.

Tickets purchased remotely are delivered as email scans; paper stubs are available at
meetings. Good luck!
